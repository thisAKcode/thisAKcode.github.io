Small Goodies¶

Broadcasting¶

This is about how matrices of different can be combined and what to do when they can't.

Rule 1
a = np.arange(3)
M = np.ones((2, 3))
a [0 1 2]
shp_a (3,)
M [[1. 1. 1.]
 [1. 1. 1.]]
shp_M (2, 3)

Rule 1 & 2
a = np.arange(3).reshape((3, 1))  
b = np.arange(3)  # [0 1 2]
"""
a is:
[[0]
 [1]
 [2]]
"""
# shp_a (3, 1)
# shp_b (3,)


a + b
#  array([[0, 1, 2],  [1, 2, 3],  [2, 3, 4]])

Rule 3

a = np.arange(3)

shp_M = (3, 2)
